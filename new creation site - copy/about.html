who we are

At New Creation Junior School, we believe every child is full of potential waiting to be discovered. Our mission is to provide all around education with a scientific orientation to fit the changing world by creating a supportive environment where curiosity, creativity, and character grow together.
We are committed to providing a strong academic foundation while also helping children develop confidence, discipline, and a love for learning that will guide them throughout their lives.
Our dedicated teachers focus on each child's unique abilities , encouraging them to explore their talents, friendships. Through engaging lessons, co-curricular activities, and strong moral guidance, we prepare our learnes not only to for academic success but also for responsibile and compassionate living.
At New Creation Junior school, education goes beyond classroom. We work with parents and the community to create a safe, inspiring space where every child can grow, dream, and become the best version of themselves.
